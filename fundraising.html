KoiBots Spring Fundraiser 2021!

Home Fundraising

Springtime is here! The KoiBots team hopes to build a competitive robot in 2022 and to do that we need your support. In order to raise money for this upcoming season, the Koibots team is launching a campaign of spring cleaning. In a socially distanced manner, outside and with masks we are available to do all sorts of chores; like spreading mulch, doing touchup painting, weeding, lawn work and more! Not only will our work help you but your donations will be used to help team members to learn and expand important 21st century skills that can help create a better world tommarrow!
